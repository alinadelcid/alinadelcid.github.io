OVERVIEW

Conceptualizing a restaurant from scratch

Nuestra Casita is a family-friendly Mexican restaurant located in Los Angeles, CA. Inspired by the colorful, vibrant culture of Mexico, the brand sought to develop an identity that was inviting, homey, and authentic. Throughout the brand, there is a consistent use of handdrawn-style typographical and illustrative elements in order to evoke the friendly and casual look that the restaurant envisioned.

MOODBOARD

Depicting a clear idea of brand tone and visuals

Throughout the design process, a moodboard was created and updated to direct a clear idea of brand visuals. The moodboard depicts the desired family-friendly tone while also showing specific connections to Mexican culture, such as colorful Mexican architecture, familism, and festivities.

LOGO

Iterations

Logo iterations were directly inspired by the ruggedness and charm of traditional Mexican homes. The top 3 iterations are seen below. The third logo was chosen as the final for its succesful combination of type while also having the logomark maintain a sketchy and loose style that evoked an authentic, friendly look.

MENU

Bi-Fold Format

The menu design incorporated fun handdrawn doodles in order to continue building the family-friendly identity while also keeping visuals cohesive with the handdrawn, sketchy style of the logo. For the actual menu book, a textured paper, cotton fastening rope, and leather menu cover were chosen to compliment the textures seen throughout the brand illustrations while also contributing to the homey, authentic feel.
