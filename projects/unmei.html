OVERVIEW

Producing cohesive print and digital deliverables

Unmei Airlines is an airline company that focuses on delivering friendly, high-standard flight experiences. Their goal is to always put the needs of their passengers first. I was tasked to establish the brands visual language through a logo design, a brochure, and a responsive landing page.

DELIVERABLES

Responsive Landing Page

The landing page is designed to be clean and cohesive. Main passenger needs were placed front and center through large, clear button options. Brand colors were implemented throughout icons, buttons, and text.

DELIVERABLES

Brochure

The brochure was designed with the same visual language from the landing page. The color choices and visual graphics (the blue curves) were inspired by the visuals and feelings when taking flight: airy, free, blue skies, and fluid motion. The brochure highlights the airline’s background, contact resources, services, and membership information.
