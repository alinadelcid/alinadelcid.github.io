OVERVIEW

Bringing awareness to an upcoming event with fun, illustrative designs

Montgomery College is a public community college in Montgomery County, Maryland. They host annual art and design exhibitions displaying student work. I was tasked to design a poster that would bring attention to the event. I also designed invitation cards.

DELIVERABLES

Poster Design (11"x17")

The purpose behind the selected color choices and illustrative approach was to create an alluring design that could be seen from afar and bring attention to the upcoming event.

DELIVERABLES

Card Design (A6)

After the poster was designed, I incorporated the same bold, enticing visual language into invitation cards that could be handed out to students, staff/faculty, or visitors on campus.
