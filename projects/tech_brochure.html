Innovate 2022: premier tech conference

I conceptualized the visual identity for Innovate 2022, a premier technology conference held at the Silicon Valley Convention Center. The client, Innovate Conferences, aims to bring together industry leaders, innovators, and tech enthusiasts to explore the latest advancements and trends in technology. The goal was to create bold and exciting brand assets that successfully presented information in an organized, legible, and visually cohesive way.
