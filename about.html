About Me :)

Hey there! I'm Alina. I am a senior graphic design student based in Maryland, currently seeking experience in graphic and UX/UI design. A bit about how I got here...

Throughout my high school career, I developed an interest for tech and design by taking a mix of fine arts and computer-science electives. It was these classes that allowed me to explore a wide range of artistic mediums while also getting my first experiences with coding. My newfound passions for art and technology led me to pursue graphic design with a focus on both the visual and UX/UI (and hopefully soon front-end web development)! I am quite happy with where I am today, in a position where I can enjoy solving complex design problems and deliver insightful visuals and experiences.

When I’m not dreaming up solutions in an Adobe file, you can probably find me taking photos of the sunset, drinking tea, watching a k-drama, scrolling through cat videos, or finding visual inspiration in the outside world. Let's connect and say hello!
