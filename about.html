About Me

I am currently a graphic design student based in Maryland. Throughout my high school career, I was able to develop my tech-creative background by taking a mix of fine arts and computer-science classes. I found myself exploring web designs, sketching portraits, fixing code, and painting landscapes all at the same time. My combined passions for art and technology brought me to where I am today, in a position where I can enjoy solving complex design problems for audiences and deliver insightful visuals and experiences.

When I’m not dreaming up solutions in an Adobe file, you can probably find me taking photos of the sunset, drinking tea, watching a k-drama, watching cat memes, or finding visual inspiration in the outside world. Any of that interest you? Let's connect!
